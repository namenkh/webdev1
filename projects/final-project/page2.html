About

Rengoku was born as the eldest son to carry on the Flame Hashira legacy in the Kyojuro household. After his mothers death, his father fell into the seduction of alcohol and constant bad temper. Despite this, Rengoku raised his little brother while pursueing the art of the Flame breathing technique. Soon, his hard work paid off and he was elevated to the Flame Hashira status. A Hashira is a status given to the demon slayers who display the best of the best in their fighting techniques.
